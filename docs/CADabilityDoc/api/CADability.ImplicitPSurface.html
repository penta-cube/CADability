ImplicitPSurface(ISurface, BoundingRect, Int32, Boolean, Int32)

Approximate the provided surface with a imlicit polynomial surface. Uses points an normals of the surface. Yields good results for quadrics (degree==2) and for ruled surfaces.

LagrangeReduction3()

Modifies the surface into it's "canonical" position. A sphere will be around the origin, a cylinder will be around the z-axis, a cone will be around the z-axis with its apex at the origin. The returned ModOp modifies the canonical form into the original form. Makes only sense for quadrics
